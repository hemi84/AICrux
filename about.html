Who we are ?

At AICrux Business Solutions, we offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to harness the power of data and AI to drive business success. Our Marketing Analytics services include Pricing Analytics, Product Analytics (such as Cohort and Funnel Analysis), Customer Analysis focusing on Lifetime Value Prediction and Churn Rate Prediction, and Market Basket Analysis. These solutions enable businesses to make data-driven marketing decisions, optimize pricing strategies, and enhance customer retention. In the realm of Supply Chain Analytics, we provide Demand Forecasting, Inventory Optimization, and Supplier Performance Analysis. These services help businesses streamline their supply chain operations, reduce costs, and improve overall efficiency. Our Operations Analytics offerings include Process Optimization, Risk Management, and Performance Metrics. These services are designed to enhance operational efficiency, mitigate risks, and track performance metrics to ensure continuous improvement. Additionally, we offer Custom AI Solutions, including AI Strategy Consulting, Machine Learning Model Development, and Data Visualization. Our custom solutions are tailored to meet the unique needs of each business, helping them leverage AI to gain actionable insights, predict trends, and visualize data in a compelling way. By integrating advanced analytics and AI into your business processes, AICrux Business Solutions empowers you to make informed decisions, optimize operations, and achieve sustainable growth.
